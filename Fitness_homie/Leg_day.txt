Leg Day (You must have a partner!):
Stretch For 15 minutes
Warm up with moderate weight until you hit 75% of your P.R.
Squat with heavy compound 6-8 reps
rest 5 minutes
10 pound less from the previous weight 6-8 reps
rest 5 minutes 
50% of the initial weight 10-12 reps
hamstring curl 2 sets of 8-10 with drop set
quad extension a set of failure 
lateral raise 2 sets of failure
